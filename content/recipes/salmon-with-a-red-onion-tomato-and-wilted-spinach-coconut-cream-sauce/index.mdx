<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 2x 200g (7oz) salmon fillets
  - 150 g (5oz) cherry tomatoes
  - 1/2 medium red onion
  - 225 g (1 cup) spinach leaves
  - 240 ml (1 cup) coconut cream
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  - 1/2 tsp dried basil
  - 1/2 tsp dried sage
  - 1/2 tsp salt
  - pinches of salt
  - 1/2 tsp black pepper
  - 1/4 tsp dried mustard powder
  - 1/8 tsp curry powder

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Quarter the tomatoes.
  - Finely slice the onion.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Heat the olive oil in a frying pan over medium heat. (I use number 5).
  - **Step 2**
    - Add the onion and tomatoes and fry until they begin to turn soft.
    - Push the onion and tomatoes to the outside of the pan. 
  - **Step 3**
    - Dry the salmon with kitchen paper and season with salt on all sides.
    - Add the salmon fillets to the centre of the pan.
    - Fry the salmon fillets for 4 mins on each side.
    - Push the salmon to the outside of the pan. 
  - **Step 4**
    - Bring the onion and tomatoes back to the centre of the pan.
    - Add the crushed garlic, basil, sage, salt, black pepper, mustard powder and curry powder. 
    - Stir the herbs into the onions and tomatoes for 1 minute.
    - Add the coconut cream and spinach and immediately reduce the heat to low. (I use number 3.)
  - **Step 5**
    - Stir everything together, be careful not to break up the salmon fillets. 
    - Simmer on low for 5-10 mins until the spinach starts to wilt.
  - **Step 6**
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
