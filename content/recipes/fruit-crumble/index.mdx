<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - **For the fruit**
    - 500g fruit
    - 50g caster sugar
  - **For the crumble**
    - 300g plain flour
    - 150g cold butter
    - 75g demarara sugar

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Preheat a fan-assisted oven to 180 deg C (350 deg F). 
  - **Step 2**
    - Mix the fruit and caster sugar in a 9inch x 9inch (23cm x 23cm) oven dish.
  - **Step 3**
    - In a bowl, add the crumble ingredients together.
  - **Step 4**
    - Rub the ingredients between your hands until the butter is combined. The ingredients will feel crumbly.
  - **Step 5**
    - Bake in the oven for 30 minutes.

</Instructions>
