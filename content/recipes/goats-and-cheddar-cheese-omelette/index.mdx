<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 3 x eggs
  - 1/8 tsp salt
  - 50g goats' cheese
  - 25g cheddar cheese
  - 1 tsp butter

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Grate the cheddar cheese.
  - Grate the goats' cheese.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Melt the butter in a medium frying pan over a medium heat. (I use number 6.)
  - **Step 2**
    - In a small bowl, mix the eggs and salt.
  - **Step 3**
    - When the butter is hot, tip the pan so the butter voers all of the bottom.
    - Pour in the eggs and again, so the mixture spreads.
    - Cook for about 5 mins, until the top of the eggs isi just set.
  - **Step 4**
    - Use a fish slice and turn the omelette over.
    - Add the cheddar cheese and goats' cheese to oner side of the omelette.
    - Leave the omelette to cook for 2 minutes.
  - **Step 5**
    - Use a fish slice and fold one half of the omelette over.
    - Leave to cook for 1 minute.
  - **Step 6**
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
