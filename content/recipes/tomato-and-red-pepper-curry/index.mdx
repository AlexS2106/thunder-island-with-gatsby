<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 200 g (7oz) cherry tomatoes
  - 200 g (70z) red bell pepper
  - 120 g (1/2 medium) fennel
  - 2 tbsp olive oil
  - 1 tsp olive oil
  - 3 cloves garlic
  - 1 tsp tomato puree
  - 1.5 tbsp fresh ginger
  - 1/2 tsp dried ground cumin
  - 1/2 tsp dried ground turmeric
  - 1/2 tsp dried ground coriander
  - 1/2 tsp salt
  - 1/2 tsp black pepper

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Halve the tomatoes.
  - Dice the pepper.
  - Finely chop the fennel.
  - Crush the garlic.
  - Grate the ginger.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Preheat a fan-assisted oven to 200 deg C or 400 deg F.
  - **Step 2**
    - Mix the cherry tomatoes, red bell pepper, fennel and olive oil in a casserole dish.
    - Bake in the oven for 30 mins, stirring half way through.
  - **Step 3**
    - About 20 mins through oven cooking, heat the lard on the hob in a frying pan on a medium-high heat. (I use number 7.)
  - **Step 4**
    - Add the garlic, tomato puree and fresh ginger to the frying pan.
    - Stir for 3 mins.
    - Add the cumin, turmeric, coriander, salt and black pepper.
    - Stir for 2 mins.
  - **Step 5**
    - Remove the casserole dish from the oven. - Stir the spice mix in with the tomato, fennel and bell pepper in the casserole dish.
    - Place the casserole dish back into the oven.
    - Bake for a further 10 mins.
  - **Step 6**
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
