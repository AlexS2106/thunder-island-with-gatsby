<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 1 small orange
  - 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  - 1/2 tsp salt
  - 1/2 tsp black pepper

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Peel and de-pip the orange.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Put the orange through a blender or food processor.
    - Alternatively use a juicer, if you don't want the bits.
  - **Step 2**
    - Mix all the ingredients.
  - **Step 3**
    - Pour over the salad and toss.
  - **Step 4**
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
