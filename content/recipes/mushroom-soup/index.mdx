<Ingredients>

 ### Ingredients
    
  - 75 g (1 cup) mushrooms
  - 115 g (1/2 medium) fennel 
  - 2 tbsp olive oil
  - 250 ml (1 cup) coconut cream (1 cup)
  - 240 ml (1 cup) beef stock

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Finely chop the fennel.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Heat the olive oil in a medium frying pan over a medium heat. (I use number 6.)
  - **Step 2**
    - Fry the fennel until it starts to brown, stir often.
  - **Step 3**
    - Add the mushrooms and fry until they are soft.
  - **Step 4**
    - Add the beef stock cube and the coconut cream and stir in.
  - **Step 5**
    - Turn the heat down to low until the sauce is barely simmering.
    - Leave to simmer for 20 mins.
  - **Step 6**
    - Divide the mushroom sauce into 2 halves.
    - Puree one half of the sauce with a food processor or hand held blender.
    - Stir the two halves back together again.
  - **Step 7**
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
