### Ingredients
    
  - 400 g (3/4 lb) de-shelled prawns
  - 2 medium tomatoes
  - 2 medium avocados
  - 6 tbsp light olive oil
  - 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  - 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  - 2 cloves garlic
  - 1/4 tsp salt
  - 1/4 tsp black pepper

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation
  - Crush the garlic
  - Peel, de-stone and dice the avocado.
  - Dice the tomtoes.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - In small bowl, mix the olive oil, apple cider vinegar, Dijon mustard, garlic, salt and pepper to make the vinaigrette.
  
  - **Step 2**
    - In a salad bowl, mix the tomatoes, avocados, prawns and vinaigrette together.
  - **Step 3**
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
