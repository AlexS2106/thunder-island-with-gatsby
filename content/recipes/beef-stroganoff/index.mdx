<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 400 g (14oz) sirloin steak
  - 1 fennel
  - 150 g (1 1/2 cups) mushrooms
  - 2 cloves of garlic
  - 300 ml (1 cup) coconut cream
  - 2 tbsp lard
  - 1 beef stock cube
  - 1/2 tbsp white vinegar
  - 1/2 tsp salt
  - 1/4 tsp black pepper
  - 1/8 tsp mustard powder

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation
  - Cut the sirloin steak into strips.
  - Finely chop the fennel.
  - Slice the mushrooms.
  - Crush the garlic.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Melt the lard in a large frying pan over medium-high heat. (I use number 8.)
  - **Step 2**
    - Cook the strips for 1 minute on each side or until just cooked. 
    - Remove from the pan and set aside.
  - **Step 3**
    - Turn the heat down to medium.
    - Fry the fennel until soft and slightly browned.
    - Add the garlic and mushrooms.
  - **Step 4**
    - Turn the heat down to low.
    - Add the coconut cream, beef stock cube, white vinegar, salt, black pepper, and dry mustard.
    - Stir in.
  - **Step 5**
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
