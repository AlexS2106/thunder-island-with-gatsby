<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 400 g carrots (around 6 medium carrots or 14oz)
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 1/2 tsp salt

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Peel, top and tail, and slice the carrots about 1cm (1/2 inch) thick.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Preheat a fan-assisted oven to 200 deg C or 400deg F.
  - **Step 2**
    - Toss the carrots in the olive oil and salt.
    - Spread the carrots out on a baking sheet on a baking tray.
  - **Step 3**
    - Roast in the oven for 25 mins
  - **Step 4**
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
