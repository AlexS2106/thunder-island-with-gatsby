<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

  - **Salad**
    - 200 g iceberg lettuce
    - 50 g broccoli florets
    - 1 medium avocado
    - 8 medium apricots
    - 50 g walnuts

  - **Mint Cream Dressing**
    - 120 ml coconut cream
    - 20 g mint leaves
    - 2 tsp lemon juice
    - large pinch of salt

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Shred the lettuce.
  - Cut the broccoli florets relatively small.
  - Dice the avocado.
  - Slice the apricots.
  - Break up the walnuts.
  - Finely chop the mint leaves.

#### Method

##### Salad 
  - **Step 1**
    - Mix the lettuce, broccoli, avocado, apricots and walnuts.

##### Dressing
  - **Step 2**
    - Mix the coconut cream, mint leaves, lemon juice and salt.
  - **Step 3**
    - Mix it all.
    
</Instructions>
