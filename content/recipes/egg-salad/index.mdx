<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 8x medium eggs
  - 1/2 cup extra light olive oil
  - 1/2 medium egg
  - 1 1/2 tsp Dijon mustard
  - 1/2 tsp white vinegar
  - 1/2 tsp salt
  - 1/4 tsp black pepper
  - 1/4 tsp sweet paprika
  - 1/4 tsp dried dill

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Place the eggs in a pan and cover them with water.
    - Place the pan on the hob and turn the hob up to the highest heat.
    - When the water starts boiling. Set a timer for 6 mins.
    - When the time is done remove from the hob.
    - Cover the eggs with cold water. Refresh the cold water if it gets warm. 
  - **Step 2**
    - Meanwhile place the olive oil and egg into a bowl.
    - Using a handheld blender whisk until the two are mixed and creamy.
    - Add the Dijon mustard, white vinegar, salt, black pepper, sweet paprika and dill and blend again.
  - **Step 3**
    - When the eggs are cold, peel the shells.
    - Mash the egg with a fork.
  - **Step 4**
    - Combine the eggs and the mayonnaise mix.
  - **Step 5**
    - Serve chilled.

</Instructions>
