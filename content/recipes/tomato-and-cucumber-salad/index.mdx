<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 5 medium tomatoes
  - 1 medium cucumber
  - 1/2 medium red onion 
  - 120 ml (1/2 cup) coconut cream
  - 2 tsp lemon juice
  - large pinch of salt

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Finely dice the tomatoes.
  - Finely dice the cucumber.
  - Finely slice the onion.
  - Chop the mint leaves very finely.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Mix the mint leaves, coconut cream, lemon juice and salt together.
  - **Step 2**
    - Mix the tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion in a large bowl.
  - **Step 3**
    - Mix the mint dressing into the salad.
  - **Step 4**
    - Serve at room temperature.

</Instructions>
