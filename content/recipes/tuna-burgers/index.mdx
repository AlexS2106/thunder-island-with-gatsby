<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 230 g (1 cup) 
  - 120 g (1/2 medium) fennel
  - 1 clove garlic
  - 2 tbsp tomato puree
  - 1 small egg
  - pinch of salt
  - pinch of black pepper

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Finely chop the fennel.
  - Drain as much liquid as you can from the tinned tuna.
  - Crush the garlic clove.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Preheat a fan-assisted oven to 180 deg C or 350 deg F.
  - **Step 2**
    - Mix the tuna, fennel, garlic, tomato puree, egg, salt and black pepper in a bowl.
  - **Step 3**
    - Cover the base of a baking tray with a baking sheet.
    - Take small amounts of the mixture.
    - Squeeze out any excess moisture.
    - Shape them into small patties about 2-3 inches (5-7 cm) in diameter.
    - Place each burger carefully on the paper.
  - **Step 4**
    - Bake in the oven for 20 mins.
  - **Step 5**
    - Serve hot or cold.

</Instructions>
