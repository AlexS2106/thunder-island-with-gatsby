<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

  - 130 g (1 medium) sweet potato
  - 110 g (1 small can or 3.5oz) undrained tin of tuna in olive oil
  - 1/2 green bell pepper
  - 1/2 medium onion
  - 2 tsp smoked paprika
  - 1/2 tsp thyme
  - 1/4 tsp garlic powder

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation
  - Peel and dice the sweet potato.
  - Roughly dice the green bell pepper.
  - Roughly dice the onion.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Preheat a fan-assisted oven to 200 deg C or 400 deg F.
  - **Step 2**
    - Mix all the ingredients in a 22cm x 22cm casserole dish (9" x 9"). Ensure the olive oil from the tinned tuna covers all the vegetables.
    - Place the casserole dish in the oven.
  - **Step 3**
    - Bake for 25 mins.
    - Stir well halfway through.
  - **Step 4**
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
