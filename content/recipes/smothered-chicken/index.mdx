<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 4 x 175g (medium) chicken breasts
  - 50 g (½ cup) mushrooms 
  - 75 g (½ cup) bell peppers, mixed 
  - 60 g (¼ of a medium) fennel
  - 2 tbsp ghee
  - ½ cup coconut
  - ½ cup chicken stock
  - 2 cloves garlic
  - ½ tbsp Dijon mustard
  - ½ tbsp apple cider vinegar
  - 1 tsp dried rosemary
  - 1 tsp salt
  - 1 tsp black pepper
    
</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Remove the chicken from the fridge about 30 mins before cooking. 
  - Slice the mushrooms
  - Slice the bell peppers.
  - Finely chop the fennel.
  - Crush the garlic.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Pat dry each chicken breast and rub with salt and pepper.
  - **Step 2**
    - Melt the ghee in a large frying pan over medium-high heat. (I use number 7.)
    - When the ghee is hot, add the chicken, and cover. Fry 5 minutes.
    - Turn the chicken, recover and fry for another 5 minutes.
    - Remove the chicken from the pan, set aside and cover.
  - **Step 3**
    - Add the fennel and bell pepper to the same pan and cook till soft, around 10 minutes.
  - **Step 4**
    - Add the mushrooms and garlic. Fry till the mushrooms soften, about 2 minutes.
  - **Step 5**
    - Add the chicken stock, coconut cream, vinegar, and rosemary. Stir everything together.
  - **Step 6**
    - Turn the heat to low and return the chicken to the pan.
    - Cover and simmer for 20 minutes.
  - **Step 7**
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
