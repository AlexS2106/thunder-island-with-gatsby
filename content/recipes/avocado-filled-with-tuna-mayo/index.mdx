<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 1 medium avocado
  - 3 tbsp tinned (canned) tuna
  - 2 tsp mayonnaise

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Drain the tuna.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Cut the avocado in half and remove the stone.
  - **Step 2**
    - In a small bowl mix the tuna and mayonnaise together.
    - Spoon the tuna mayonnaise into the avocado.
  - **Step 3**
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
