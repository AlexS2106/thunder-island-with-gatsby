<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

  - **For the burger**
    - 500 g (1 lb) beef mince
    - 100 g (½ large) yellow onion 
    - 1 x medium egg
    - 25 g (¼ cup) almond flour
    - ½ tsp salt
    - ½ tsp black pepper
    - ½ tsp mustard powder
    - ½ tbsp lard
  - **For the tangy burger sauce**
    - 5 tbsp mayonnaise
    - 1 tbsp tomato puree
    - 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Very finely slice the onion.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Mix all the ingredients for the tangy burger sauce and place in the fridge.
  - **Step 2**
    - In a large bowl, mix the beef mince, onion, egg, almond flour, salt, pepper and mustard powder.
    - Squish the burger mixture through your fingers until the texture becomes smooth and soft.
    - Make four large burgers from the mixture about 2cm (¾ inch) thick.
  - **Step 3**
    - Melt the lard in a large frying pan on a medium high heat. (I use number 7.)
    - Using a fish slice, slide all the burgers into the pan and fry for 3½ minutes.
    - Carefully, turn the burger over and fry for another 3½ minutes.
  - **Step 4**
    - Serve the beefburgers with the tangy burger sauce on top.

</Instructions>
