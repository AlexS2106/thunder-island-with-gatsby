<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 4 x 225g (1/2 lb each) pork medallions
  - 75 g (1/2 cup) mushrooms
  - 50 g (â…“ cup) onion
  - 35 ml (1/8 cup) beef stock
  - 60 ml (1/4 cup) coconut cream
  - 2 tbsp ghee
  - 1 clove garlic
  - 2 tsp salt
  - 2 tsp black pepper
  - 3 tbsp almond flour

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Remove the medallions from the fridge about 30 mins before cooking. 
  - Slice the mushrooms
  - Chop the onion.
  - Crush the garlic.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Pat the medallions dry.
    - Sprinkle the salt and black pepper on all sides.
    - Sprinkle the almond flour equally on all four sides, then pat the flour into the meat.
  - **Step 2**
    -  Melt the ghee into a medium-sized frying pan on medium-high heat. (I use number 7.)
  - **Step 3**
    - When the ghee is bubbling, add the pork.
    - Cook for 3 minutes on each side. 
    - Remove the pork from the pan, set aside and cover.
  - **Step 4**
    - Add the onions and mushrooms to the same pan. 
    - Cook till soft, less than 5 minutes.
    - Add all the remaining ingredients and stir thoroughly.
  - **Step 5**
    - Turn the heat down to low. (I use number 3.)
    - Re-add the pork medallions and spoon the sauce to cover the meat thoroughly.
    - Cover the pan and leave to simmer gently for 20 minutes.
  - **Step 6**
    - Serve with the sauce poured over the top of the pork medallions. 

</Instructions>
