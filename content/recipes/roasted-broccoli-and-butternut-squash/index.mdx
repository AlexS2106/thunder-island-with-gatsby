<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 450 g (1/2 medium) butternut squash
  - 60 g (1 medium) tomato
  - 100 g (1/2 medium head) broccoli
  - 40 g (3X) spring onions
  - 1 tsp curry powder of preferred strength
  - 1/2 tsp salt
  - 2 tbsp olive oil

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Peel, de-seed and dice the butternut squash.
  - Chop the broccoli into small florets.
  - Dice the tomato.
  - thickly slice the spring onions

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Preheat a fan-assisted oven to 200 deg C (400 deg F).
  - **Step 2**
    - Mix all ingredients together.
    - Place everything into a large casserole dish.
    - Roast in the oven for 40 mins. 
    - Stir part of the way through.
  - **Step 3**
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
