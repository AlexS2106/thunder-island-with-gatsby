<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 180 g (1 1/2 cups) flour
  - 175ml (3/4 cup) warm water
  - 1 tsp salt
  - 1 tsp active yeast

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - If you have a non-stick loaf tray, skip this step; if you don't, grease a baking sheet and line the tray with the sheet.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Prepare a bread tray of about 9 inches in length.    
  - **Step 2**
    - Mix the flour, salt and yeast in a bowl.
    - Add the water and mix in.
  - **Step 3**
    - Leave in a warm place to rise for 1 hour.
  - **Step 4**
    - Preheat a fan-assisted oven to 180 deg C or 350 deg C.
  - **Step 5**
    - Bake the bread for 45 minutes.
  - **Step 6**
    - Let it cool a little before slicing.

</Instructions>
