<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
  
  - 1 medium courgette per person

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation
  - Top, tail and peel the courgette.
  - Spiralise the courgette.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Place the spiralised courgette on a plate. -  Microwave on high for 2-4 mins until heated through, 'fluff' the courgette part way through.
  - **Step 2**
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
