<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
  - 400 g (14oz) cauliflower florets
  - 2 tbsp butter
  - 1/4 tsp salt
  - 50 g (1.7oz) cheddar cheese
  - 2 slices bacon

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Chop the cauliflower into florets.
  - Grate the cheese.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Boil some water in a saucepan.
    - When it's boiling, add the cauliflower and cook for 20 minutes until soft, drain.
  - **Step 2**
    - In a food processor, add the cooked cauliflower, butter and salt.
    - Blitz until smooth.
  - **Step 3**
    - Turn the grill on high.
  - **Step 4**
    - Spoon the cauliflower into an oven-proof dish of approx. 15cm by 15cm (6 inches by 6 inches).
    - Sprinkle the cheese on top.
    - Place under the grill for 5-10 minutes until the cheese is bubbling and slightly browned.
  - **Step 5**
    - While grilling, place the bacon on a microwaveable plate and cook to desired 'doneness'.
    - Cut the cooked bacon into small pieces.
  - **Step 6**
    - Sprinkle the bacon bits onto the cheese and serve.

</Instructions>
