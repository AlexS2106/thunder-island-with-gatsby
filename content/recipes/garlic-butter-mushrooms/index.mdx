<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 225 g (about 3 cups) mushrooms
  - 3 tbsp butter
  - 1 tbsp crushed garlic
  - 1 tsp dried parsley
  - 1/2 tsp salt
  - 1/4 tsp black pepper

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Slice the mushrooms.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Melt the butter in a frying pan over a medium heat.
  - **Step 2**
    - Add all the remaining ingredients to the frying pan.
  - **Step 3**
    - Fry for 20 mins, stir often.
  - **Step 4**
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
