<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 180 g (1x medium) apple
  - 130 g (1x medium) sweet potato
  - 70g (1x medium) parsnip
  - 2 tbsp honey
  - 2 tbsp olive oil
  - 1/2 tsp salt
  - 1/4 tsp black pepper
  - 1/8 tsp ground cinnamon

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation
  - Peel, de-core and roughly chop the apple.
  - Peel and dice the sweet potato.
  - Peel, top and tail and dice the parsnip.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Preheat a fan-assisted oven to 200 deg C or 400 deg F.
  - **Step 2**
    - Line a baking tray with baking paper and pour the vegetables onto it.
  - **Step 3**
    - Mix the olive oil, salt, black pepper and ground cinnamon in a small bowl.
    - Pour the oil mixture onto the vegetables and mix.
    - Spread the vegetables out evenly on the tray.
    - Drizzle the honey over the vegetables.
  - **Step 4**
    - Roast in the oven for 30 mins.
    - Turn halfway through the cooking time.
  - **Step 5**
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
