<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 60 ml (1/2 cup) mayonnaise
  - 50 g cheddar cheese
  - 2 cloves garlic
  - 1 tsp Dijon mustard

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Crush the garlic
  - Grate the cheese

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Add all the ingredients to a small bowl and mix well.
  - **Step 2** 
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
