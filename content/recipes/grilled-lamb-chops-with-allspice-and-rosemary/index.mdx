<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 4x 145g (large) lamb chops, bone in
  - 60 ml (1/4 cup) olive oil
  - 2 cloves garlic
  - 2 tbsp lemon juice
  - 1 tsp salt
  - 1 tsp allspice
  - 1/2 tsp black pepper
  - 1/2 tsp dried rosemary

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Marinate the meat for 5 hours - Step 1.
  - Crush the garlic. 

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Mix the olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, salt, allspice, black pepper and rosemary together.
    - Add in the lamb chops and leave in the fridge to marinate. 
    - Marinate for at least 5 hours.
  - **Step 2**
    - Preheat the grill to medium high.
  - **Step 3**
    - Place the lamb chops onto some aluminium foil on a tray.
    - Grill 5 mins on one side. Flip over and grill 5 mins on the other side.
  - **Step 4**
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
