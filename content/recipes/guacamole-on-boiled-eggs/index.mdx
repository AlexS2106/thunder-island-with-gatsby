<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 4 x eggs
  - 1 x avocado
  - 1 x tomato
  - 1 x spring onion 
  - 1 x garlic clove
  - 1/2 tsp olive oil
  - 1/4 tsp lemon juice
  - 1/4 tsp lime juice
  - pinch of sweet paprika
  - pinch of salt

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Peeled and de-stone the avocado.
  - Finely chop the tomato and the onion.
  - Crush the garlic.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Place all four eggs in a saucepan of cold water. The water should cover the eggs.
    - Place the pan on the hob and turn the hob up high.
    - Bring the water to a boil and set a timer for 7 mins.
  - **Step 2**
    - Using a fork or a food processor, make the avocado smooth.
    - Mix in all of the remaining ingredients except for the sweet paprika.
  - **Step 3**
    - When the 7 mins are up, immediately run the eggs under cold water until they feel cool.
    - De-shell the eggs.
  - **Step 4**
    - Slice the eggs, divide them in half and arrange the slices on two plates.
    - Sprinkle each portion with a pinch of salt.
  - **Step 5**
    - Spoon the guacamole over the boiled egg slices.
    - Sprinkle the sweet paprika over each guacamole and eggs portion.
  - **Step 6**
    - Serve chilled.

</Instructions>
