<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 1 cup extra light olive oil
  - 1 medium egg
  - 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  - 1 tsp white vinegar
  - 1/4 tsp salt

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Place the olive oil and egg into a bowl.
    - Use a handheld blender and whisk until the two are mixed and creamy.
  - **Step 2**
    - Add the remaining ingredients and briefly blend again.
  - **Step 3**
    - Keep chilled until serving.

</Instructions>
