<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 2 medium courgettes
  - 1 small tomato
  - 30 g (1oz) apple
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 2 pinches dried basil

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Peel and decore the apple.
  - Wash the courgette skins, then top and tail.
  - Finely dice the tomato.
  - Finely dice the apple

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Preheat a fan-assisted oven to 200 deg C or 400 deg F.
  - **Step 2**
    - Cut the courgettes in half length ways.
    - Scoop out the flesh in the center making sure to leave enough of the flesh at either end to keep it as a boat.
    - Keep the removed flesh.
  - **Step 3**
    - Cover all sides of the courgettes with the olive oil.
    - Place each boat on baking paper on a baking tray.
  - **Step 4**
    - Mix the tomato, apple and courgette flesh together.
    - Spoon the mix back into the courgette boats.
    -Sprinkle a pinch of basil onto each boat.
  - **Step 5**
    - Bake in the oven for 20 mins.
  - **Step 6**
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
