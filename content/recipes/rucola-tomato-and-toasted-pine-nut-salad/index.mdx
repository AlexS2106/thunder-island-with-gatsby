<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 100 g (3 1/2 oz) rucola
  - 6 medium tomatoes
  - 1 tbsp pine nuts
  - 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - 1 tbsp lemon juice
  - 1/4 tsp salt
  - 1/8 tsp black pepper
  - 1/8 tsp dried mustard powder

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Roughly rip up the rucola.
  - Dice the tomatoes.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Place the pine nuts in a frying pan over a medium heat. (I use number 6.)
    - Stir often to prevent the pine nuts from over-browning or burning on one side.
    - In less than 10 minutes they will look slightly browned.
    - Remove from the heat.
  - **Step 2**
    - In a small bowl, mix the olive oil, lemon juice, salt, pepper and mustard powder.
  - **Step 3**
    - Mix the rucola, tomatoes and lemon vinaigrette together.
    - Sprinkle the pine nuts over the top of the salad.
  - **Step 4**
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
