<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 2 X 1.5kg (3-4 lb each) pork spare ribs 
  - 125 ml (1/2 cup) honey 
  - 60 ml (1/4 cup) white vinegar 
  - 2 cloves garlic
  - 2 tsp tomato puree
  - 2 tsp sweet paprika
  - 1 tsp baking soda
  - 1/2 tsp salt
  - 1/8 tsp dried ground ginger
  - pinch of black pepper

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Allow time to marinate. (See step 1.)

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Rub the salt onto all sides of the pork spare ribs.
    - Cut the ribs every 2-3 ribs.
    - Mix all the remaining ingredients in a large bowl.
    - Place the ribs in the bowl and mix everything up; ensure the mixture completely covers all sides of the meat.
    - Cover the bowl and leave it in the fridge for 1 hour.
  - **Step 2**
    - Preheat a fan-assisted oven to 190 deg C or (375 deg F).
  - **Step 3**
    - Line the bottom of a baking tray with 2-3 layers of thick aluminium foil.
    - Place the ribs meat side down and flat on the baking tray.
    - Pour any leftover marinade over the ribs.
    - Use another sheet of aluminium foil to cover the ribs in the tray.
  - **Step 4**
    - Cook the ribs in the oven for 1 hour, turning the ribs every 15 mins.
    - Remove the covering layer of aluminium foil at 30 mins.
  - **Step 5**
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
