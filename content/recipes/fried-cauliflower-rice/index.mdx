<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 200 g cauliflower (about 1/4 head of a medium cauliflower)
  - 1 tsp lard (or use an acceptable alternative for vegetarian)

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Place the cauliflower in a food processor and pulse until it resembles fine grains.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Melt the lard in a medium frying pan over a medium heat. (I use number 7.)
  - **Step 2**
    - when the lard is hot, pour in the cauliflower rice.
    - Fry the cauliflower for 10 mins stirring often.
  - **Step 3**
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
