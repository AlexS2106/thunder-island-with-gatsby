<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 1 medium red bell pepper
  - 1/4 tsp olive oil

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Pre-heat a fan-assisted oven to 200 deg C or 400 deg F.
  - **Step 2**
    - Cut the red bell pepper in half.
    - Rub both halves with the oil.
    - Place the halves cut-side down on a baking sheet on a baking tray.
    - Roast for 25 mins.
  - **Step 3**
    - After the bell pepper has cooled enough, gently remove the stalk and pips.
  - **Step 4**
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
