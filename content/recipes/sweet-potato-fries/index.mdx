<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 300 g (approx 2 medium sized) sweet potatoes 
  - 2 tbsp olive oil
  - 1/2 tsp salt
  - 1/4 tsp black pepper

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Peel the sweet potato and chop into fries.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Preheat a fan-assisted oven to 200 deg C (400 deg F).
  - **Step 2**
    - Mix together the sweet potato fries, olive oil, salt and pepper.
    - Spread the sweet potato fries out on baking paper on a baking tray.
  - **Step 3**
    - Bake for 25 mins turning half way through.
  - **Step 4**
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
