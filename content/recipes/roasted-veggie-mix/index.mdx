<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 235 g (1x medium) fennel
  - 150 g (1x medium head) broccoli
  - 200 g (2x medium) courgette
  - 120 g (1x medium) red bell pepper
  - 370 g (3x medium) tomatoes
  - 2 tsp tomato puree
  - 1 clove garlic
  - 2 tsp dried parsley
  - 2 tsp dried basil
  - 1/4 tsp salt
  - 1/4 tsp black pepper
  - 60 ml (1/4 cup) chicken stock or vegetable stock for vegetarian
  - 60 ml (1/4 cup) olive oil 

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Roughly chop (not too small) the fennel, broccoli, courgette and bell pepper.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Preheat fan-assisted oven to 200 deg C (400 deg F).
  - **Step 2**
    - In a food processor or blender mix the tomatoes, tomato puree, garlic, parsley, basil, salt, black pepper, chicken stock and olive oil.
  - **Step 3**
    - Mix all the vegetables and the liquid together in a large casserole dish.
  - **Step 4**
    - Cook for 1 hour stirring half way through.
  - **Step 5**
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
