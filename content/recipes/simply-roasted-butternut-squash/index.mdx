<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 1 medium butternut squash
  - 1-2 tbsp olive oil
  - 1/2 tsp salt

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Peel and de-seed the butternut squash.
  - Cut it into cubes about an inch square.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Pre-heat a fan-assisted oven to 200 deg C or 400 deg F.
  - **Step 2**
    - Mix the butternut squash cubes, the olive oil and the salt together.
    - Spread the squash cube out on baking paper on a baking tray.
    - Roast for about 35 mins turning the squash cubes about half way through.
  - **Step 3**
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
