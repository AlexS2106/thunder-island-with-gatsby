<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 2x mahi mahi fillets
  - 1 tbsp lard
  - 1 tsp dried parsley
  - 1/4 tsp salt
  - 1/4 tsp black pepper
  - 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  - 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper 

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Remove the mahi mahi from the fridge about 30 mins before cooking to warm up.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Dry the mahi mahi by patting it with kitchen paper.
    - Mix all the above spices in a bowl and rub on both sides of each mahi mahi.
  - **Step 2**
    - Heat the lard in a medium frying pan on a medium high heat. (I use number 7.)
  - **Step 3**
    - Place the spiced mahi mahi skin side down and cook for 4 mins.
    - Flip over and cook for another 4 mins.
    - When the mahi mahi is cooked remove it from the heat.
    - A meat thermometer will read 52 deg C or 125 deg F.
  - **Step 4**
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
