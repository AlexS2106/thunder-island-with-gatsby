<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 2x 175g (medium-sized) chicken breasts
  - 1/2 egg, beaten
  - 65 g (1 cup) cauliflower
  - 1 tbsp lard
  - 1 tsp sweet paprika
  - 1 tsp smoked paprika
  - 1 tsp salt
  - 1 tsp black pepper
  - 1/2 tsp ground allspice
  - 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
  - 1/4 tsp ground ginger
  - 1/4 tsp dried ground mustard

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - In a food processor blitz the caulfilower into rice.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Mix the sweet paprika, smoked paprika, salt, black pepper, allspice, nutmeg, ginger, mustard on a plate.
    - Place the beaten egg on another plate.
    - Place the cauliflower rice on a third plate.
  - **Step 2**
    - Beat the chicken until it is no more than 2cm (3/4 inch) in thickness.
    - Dip the chicken into the herb mix one side then the other.
    - Dip each chicken breast into the egg one side then the other.
    - Dip the chicken into the cauliflower mix one side and then the other. 
    - Use your fingers to put some more cauliflower any sparse areas.
  
  - **Step 3**
    - Melt the lard on a medium high heat (I use number 7).
    - When the lard is hot fry the chicken for 5 minutes on each side. 
    - Try not to move the chicken around the pan to much.
    - When the chicken is cooked through, remove from the heat.
  - **Step 4**
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
