<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 1 head broccoli
  - 2 tbsp olive oil
  - 1 tsp salt

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Chop the broccoli into bite size florets.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Preheat a fan-assisted oven to 200 deg C or 400 deg F.
  - **Step 2**
    - Mix the broccoli with the olive oil and salt.
  - **Step 3**
    - Spread out the florets on baking paper on a baking tray.
    - Roast for 25 mins turning half-way through.
  - **Step 4**
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
