<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 300 g (10oz) cabbage 
  - 115 g (1/2 medium) fennel
  - 3 slices bacon
  - 1 clove garlic
  - 1 tbsp lard
  - 1 tsp salt
  - 1/4 tsp black pepper
  - 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  - 1/2 tsp smoked paprika

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Crush the garlic.
  - Finely chop the fennel.
  - Roughly slice the cabbage.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Melt the lard in a large frying pan on a medium high heat. (I use number 7.)
  - **Step 2**
    - Fry the bacon till crispy, remove and set aside.
  - **Step 3**
    - Add the fennel to the pan and fry till soft, about 15 mins.
  - **Step 4**
    - Add the cabbage, garlic, salt, black pepper, garlic powder and sweet paprika to the pan.
  - **Step 5**
    - Turn the heat down to medium low. (I use number 3.)
    - Chop the bacon up small and return it to the frying pan.
    - Simmer on a low heat medium heat for 30 mins stirring often.
    - Add more lard if the cabbage starts to stick.
  - **Step 6**
    - 5 minutes before the end, stir in the coconut cream.
  - **STep 7**
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
