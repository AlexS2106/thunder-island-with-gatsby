<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 2 large parsnips
  - 3 tbsp olive oil
  - 1.5 tsp salt
  - 1 tsp curry powder

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Peel, top and tail, and slice the parsnips into 2-3cm (1 inch) cubes.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Pre-heat a fan-assisted oven to 200 deg C or 400 deg C.
  - **Step 2**
    - Mix the oil, salt and curry powder in a bowl and toss the parsnips until each slice is thoroughly covered.
  - **Step 3**
    - Spread out the parsnip chips on baking paper on a baking tray.
    - Bake for 20 mins.
    - Toss the parsnip chips halfway through cooking.
  - **Step 4**
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
