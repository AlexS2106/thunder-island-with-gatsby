<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 400 g (14oz) shrimp
  - 1/2 tbsp lard
  - 2 tsp smoked paprika
  - 1/2 tsp salt
  - 1/2 tsp dried chives
  - 1/4 tsp dried basil
  - 1/4 tsp dried thyme
  - 1/4 tsp black pepper
  - 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  - 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper (use more/less or omit entirely if desired)

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Mix the blackening spices, smoked paprika, garlic, salt, chives, basil, thyme, black pepper and cayenne pepper, in a bowl.
    - Mix the shrimp into the blackening spices. Try to cover all the sides of each shrimp.
  - **Step 2**
    - Melt the lard on a medium to high heat. (I use number 8.)
  - **Step 3**
    - When the lrd is hot, fry the shrimp for about 2 mins on each side until cooked.
    - They should turn pinkish when done.
  - **Step 4**
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
