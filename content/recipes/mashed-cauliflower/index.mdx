<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 400 g (14oz) cauliflower
  - 2 tbsp ghee (or butter)
  - 1/4 tsp salt

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation
  - Roughly chop the cauliflower.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Boil the cauliflower florets for about 25 mins until soft.
    - Drain off the water.
  - **Step 2**
    - Put the boiled cauliflower, the ghee and the salt into a food processor.
    - Pulse until it resembles mashed potato.
  - **Step 3**
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
