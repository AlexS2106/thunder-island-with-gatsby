<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
  
  - **For The chicken**
    - 800 g (1 1/2 lb) chicken breast
    - 1 tsp salt
    - 1 tsp black pepper
    - 2 tsp Dijon mustard
    - 4 tbsp olive oil

  - **The Salad**
    - 300 g (1x large) cucumber
    - 1 medium green apple 
    - 2 medium green bell peppers

  - **The Pine Nuts**
    - 50 g (1 1/2oz) pine nuts

  - **The Dijon Mustard Vinaigrette**
    - 6 tbsp light olive oil
    - 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
    - 2 tsp Dijon mustard
    - 2 cloves garlic
    - 1/4 tsp salt
    - 1/4 tsp black pepper

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Finely slice the cucumber.
  - Peel, de-core and dice the apple.
  - De-seed and dice the green peppers.
  - Crush the garlic.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Preheat a fan-assisted oven to 200 deg C.
  - **Step 2**
    - Prepare a baking tray with baking paper.
  - **Step 3**
    - Mix the salt black pepper, Dijon mustard and olive oil.
  - **Step 4**
    - Beat the chicken breasts to about 2-3 cm thickness (between 1 and 1.5 inches).
    - Dip the chicken into the mustard mixture. - Make sure both sides are covered.
    - Place the chicken onto the baking paper and pour any excess mixture over the top.
  - **Step 5**
    - Cook in the oven for 20 mins till juices run clear.
  - **Step 6**
    - While the chicken is cooking, place the pine nuts in a frying pan over a medium heat.
    - Stir often to prevent the nuts over-browning or burning on one side.
    - Within 10 mins or less, they will be slightly browned.
    - Remove from the heat and set aside.
  - **Step 7**
    - In a small bowl mix the vinaigrette ingredients.
  - **Step 8**
    - Add the salad ingredients to a large bowl.
  - **Step 9**  
    - When the chicken is cooked, cut it into bite-sized pieces.
    - Add the chicken to the salad and mix all the ingredients together.
  - **Step 10**
    - Pour the vinaigrette over the salad first, then add the pine nuts.
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
