<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 15-20 spring onions
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 1/4 tsp salt

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Top, tail and peel off the outer layer of the spring onions.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Preheat a fan-assisted oven to 180 deg C or 350 deg F.
  - **Step 2**
    - Mix the spring onions, olive oil and salt together.
  - **Step 3**
    - Spread the spring onions on baking paper on a baking tray.
    - Roasted for 25 mins, turn them halfway through cooking.
  - **Step 4**
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
