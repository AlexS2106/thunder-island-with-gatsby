<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 340 g (1 can or 12oz) corned beef 
  - 5 medium eggs
  - ½ tbsp lard
  - ¼ tsp salt
  - ½ tsp mustard powder

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation
  - Dice the corned beef

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Heat the lard in a large frying pan on a medium heat. (I use number 6.)
  - **Step 2**
    - When the lard is heated, add the corned beef, salt and mustard powder.
    - Fry for 10 minutes, stirring often.
  - **Step 3**
    - Crack the eggs in with the beef. 
    - Keep stirring until the eggs have gone solid, about 3-4 minutes.
  - **Step 4**
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
