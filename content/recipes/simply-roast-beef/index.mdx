<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 1 kg (about 2.2lb) top side beef joint
  - 1/2 tsp salt

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>
  
### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Remove the joint from the fridge about 1 hour before cooking. 

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Preheat the oven to 200 deg C or 400 deg F.
  - **Step 2**
    - Use kitchen paper to pat the beef dry.
    - Rub the salt on all sides of the beef.
  - **Step 3**
    - Place the beef on a roasting tray and into the oven.
    - Roast for 20 mins.
  - **Step 4**
    - After 20 mins, turn the oven down to 160 deg C or 320 deg F.
    - Roast for a further 40 mins.
    - If you like a more 'done' beef, leave another 20 mins for medium.
    - Remove the beef from the oven. 
    - Wrap the beef in two layers of aluminium foil.
    - Leave it to rest for 10 mins.
  - **Step 5**
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
