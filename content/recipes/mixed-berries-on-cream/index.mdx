<Ingredients>

 ### Ingredients
    
  - 5 tbsp (1/4 cup) coconut cream or Greek yoghurt
  - 2 tbsp mixed berries
  - 2 tsp honey

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Chill the coconut cream.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Whisk the chilled coconut cream, if using.
  - **Step 2**
    - Pour the mixed berries on top.
  - **Step 3**
    - Drizzle on the honey.
  - **Step 4**
    - Serve chilled.

</Instructions>
