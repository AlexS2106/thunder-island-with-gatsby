<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 500 g (about 1lb)beef mince 
  - 115 g (1/2 medium) fennel 
  - 200 g (70z or 1/2 can) chopped tinned tomatoes
  - 100 g (1/2 a head) broccoli
  - 150 g (1/4 head) cauliflower
  - 120 g (1 medium) bell pepper, any colour
  - 1 tsp lard
  - 120 ml (1/2 cup) coconut cream
  - 1 chicken stock cube

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation
  - Finely chop the fennel
  - Dice the broccoli and cauliflower.
  - Slice the bell pepper.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Heat the lard in a large frying pan over a medium high heat. (I use number 7.)
  - **Step 2**
    - Fry the fennel and the bell pepper until soft, about 15 minutes.
  - **Step 3**
    - Add the beef mince to the same frying pan and fry for a further 5-10 mins until the meat is brown.
    - Turn the heat down to low.
  - **Step 4**
    - Add the tinned tomatoes, the coconut cream and the chicken stock cube.
    - Leave to simmer for 40 mins.
  - **Step 5**
    - Boil some water in a pan.
    - Add the broccoli and cauliflower.
    - Boil for about 20 mins.
  - **Step 6**
    - As soon as the broccoli and cauliflower are soft, remove from the water and add to the mince.
  - **Step 7**
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
